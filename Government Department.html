Government Department

Government departments like India's Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE) and the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) promote smart home adoption by investing in digital infrastructure and developing national policy roadmaps for energy efficiency and Internet of Things (IoT) technologies. Their initiatives include launching projects like the Smart Cities Mission and Digital India to support connected homes, foster innovation, and implement smart solutions that enhance citizen comfort, security, and energy savings.

The Smart Government System

Smart homes provide comfortable, fully controlled and secure lifestyles to their occupants. Moreover, smart homes can save energy and money with the possibility of profiting from selling clean renewable energy to the grid. On the other hand, the probable decrease in total domestic-energy loads encourages many governments to support promising smart-home technologies.
